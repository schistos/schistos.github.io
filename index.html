This site contains expression profiles of Schistosoma mansoni genes from different annotation versions.
- Expression in all life stages (v5)
- Expression in adults and gonads (v5)
- Expression in all life stages from single- or mixed-sex infection (v7)

Schisto Databases
  • WormBase Parasite: https://parasite.wormbase.org
  • GeneDB: https://www.genedb.org/
  • SchistoDB: https://schistodb.net/
  • SchistoCyte Atlas (RNAi phenotype + single cell clusters): http://www.collinslab.org/schistocyte/
  • SmlncRNAs @VerjoLab: http://schistosoma.usp.br/
  • Sm Histone ChIP-Seq data https://genome.univ-perp.fr/
