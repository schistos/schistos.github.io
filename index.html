This site contains expression profiles of Schistosoma mansoni genes from different annotation versions.
- RNA-seq data in all life stages (v5)
- RNA-seq data in adults and gonads (v5)
- RNA-seq data in all life stages from single/mixed-sex infection (v7)
- Lifecycle RNA-seq (stage- and sex-specific) (v10)
